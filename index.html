Geometry-aware motion retargeting for two-character interaction

Inseo Jang1,2, Soojin Choi1, Seokhyeon Hong1, Chaelin Kim1, Junyong Noh1
KAIST1, KAI Inc.2
Paper Video

Abstract

Interactive motion between multiple characters is widely utilized in games and movies. However, the method for generating interactive motions considering the character's diverse mesh shape has yet to be studied. We propose a novel method for geometry-aware motion retargeting for two-character interaction.

A Spatio Cooperative Transformer (SCT) is to retarget the interacting motions of two characters having arbitrary mesh connectivity. An anchor loss function for SCT is to maintain the geometric distance between the interacting characters when they are retargeted. A motion augmentation method with deformation-based adaptation is to prepare a source-target paired dataset with an identical mesh connectivity for training.
